Research

At Columbia

  • Labor Law and Employment Contracts: Using computational-linguistic tools to study the effects of labor legislation on union contract provisions. RA for Profs Suresh Naidu, Bentley MacLeod, and Elliott Ash.
  • Monopsony in Online Labor Markets: Long-term study of labor supply elasticities within the Amazon Mechanical Turk online labor market. Joint work with Suresh Naidu, Arindrajit Dube, and Siddharth Suri.

At UC Berkeley

  • Urban Accessibility Indices: Research in Urban Economics conducted as research assistant for Prof. Victor Couture in 2014 and 2015.
  • Networks of Trade in Corporate Bond Markets: Financial transaction network construction and visualization project, advised by Prof. Amir Kermani. Resulted in research assistantship credit in SSRN working paper.

At Stanford

  • Strategic Information Sharing in Village Networks: Research conducted with Prof. Arun Chandrasekhar throughout 2014. Resulted in poster presentation for CS229 (Machine Learning) final project.
  • Information Cartography: Research conducted within the Stanford Network Analysis Project (SNAP) under Prof. Jure Leskovec in 2013. Resulted in publication of "Information Cartography: Creating Zoomable, Large-Scale Maps of Information" in Proceedings of the 2013 ACM SIGKDD International Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (KDD).

At University of Maryland

  • NP-Completeness of Planar Hamiltonian Cycle: A for-credit independent study performed in the fall of 2011 with Prof. William Gasarch
  • Metaheuristic Approaches to Resource Scheduling in Heterogeneous Wireless Networks: Research performed in the summer of 2011 at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Resulted in a poster presentation at the 2011 Joint Mathematics Meetings.
  • Chronopoint: A genetic algorithm for evolving musical counterpoint, developed in the spring of 2010 with Prof. James Reggia. Resulted in a publication in Proceedings of the 2011 IEEE Congress on Evolutionary Computation
  • Parallelization of Brain-Computer Interfaces: Research performed in the summer of 2010 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign's Brain-Computer Interface lab with Prof. Todd P. Coleman
